About BizConnect

BizConnect is India's premier marketplace for business acquisitions, connecting verified buyers with quality business opportunities. We provide comprehensive due diligence, professional valuation services, and secure transaction management to ensure successful deals.

Our Mission

To create a transparent, secure, and efficient marketplace for business transactions in India, leveraging technology and professional expertise to facilitate successful business acquisitions and exits.

Why Choose Us?

  • 100% verified listings through PAN, Aadhar, GST, and MCA checks
  • Professional due diligence and valuation services
  • Secure escrow and digital contract management
  • Compliance with all Indian regulations (Companies Act, LLP Act, Income Tax, FEMA)
  • Access to verified CAs, lawyers, and business advisors
  • Confidential and secure transaction process
